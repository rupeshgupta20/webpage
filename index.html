 About Lahar Banjari According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Lahar Banjari village is 365721. Lahar Banjari village is located in Untari Road subdivision of Palamu district in Jharkhand, India. It is situated 6km away from sub-district headquarter Untari Road (tehsildar office) and 56km away from district headquarter Medininagar. As per 2009 stats, Lahar Banjari village is also a gram panchayat. The total geographical area of village is 555 hectares. Lahar Banjari has a total population of 3,324 peoples, out of which male population is 1,719 while female population is 1,605. Literacy rate of lahar banjari village is 50.60% out of which 63.00% males and 37.32% females are literate. There are about 614 houses in lahar banjari village. Pincode of lahar banjari village locality is 822116.
